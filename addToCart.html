    ABOUT THE DESIGNER

    Los Angeles-based brand Le Superbe nails the laid-back glitz of Southern California in every piece of Le Superbe clothing. Named after a street in Venice, the label brings high fashion elements to low-key styles in a way that feels both refreshing and freeing (kind of key when it comes to creating your best wardrobe), and the results speak for themselves. From sequined pencil skirts to graphic sweatshirts and Le Superbe cardigans, these are designs that make getting dressed feel fun (and effortless!) all over again.
